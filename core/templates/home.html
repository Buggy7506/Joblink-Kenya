JobLink Kenya

JobLink Kenya is a digital employment platform built to connect job seekers across Kenya with verified job opportunities and trusted employers.

The platform is designed to simplify and secure the job search process by allowing users to:

  • Create and manage professional career profiles
  • Browse and apply for job opportunities
  • Track job applications and updates
  • Access their accounts securely using Google Sign-In

Google Sign-In is used strictly for user authentication and account security. For details on how personal information is collected, used, and protected, please review our Privacy Policy.
