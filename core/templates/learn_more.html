Learn More About Information Sharing

This page helps explain how contact information may be shared on JobLink Kenya, how to protect your data, and how to get help when you need support.

1. Why Contact Information May Be Shared

On JobLink Kenya, users interact with others to connect about job opportunities, networking, and professional growth. When you upload your resume or contact details, other users may see or interact with that information as part of legitimate networking activities within the platform.

2. What This Means for Your Privacy

  • You control what contact info you share (email, phone, links).
  • Your profile settings determine who can see contact details.
  • Uploaded contact information is only visible to authenticated users.
Important: Always be cautious about sharing personal details outside the platform, and ensure you only connect with trusted individuals and employers.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect user data. Contact details are stored securely and shared only when you choose to make them visible or interact with others on the platform.

4. Getting Help & Reporting Issues

If you find that someone is misusing your contact information or behaving inappropriately, you can report the user through your account settings:

  • Go to the user’s profile.
  • Click “Report” or “Block”.
  • Provide details about the issue so our support team can review.

5. Support Tools & Account Assistance

If you need help with your account, profile settings, or data privacy, you can reach out through the support tools available in your dashboard. These tools provide guides, FAQs, and ways to contact support if you experience technical problems or account concerns.

6. Updates to This Page

We may update this page as our platform evolves, technology standards improve, or legal requirements change. Check back regularly to stay informed.
