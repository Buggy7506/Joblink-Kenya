Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how JobLink Kenya collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Introduction

JobLink Kenya ("we", "our", or "us") is an online employment platform designed to connect job seekers with employers across Kenya. We are committed to safeguarding your personal data and being transparent about how it is handled.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Account role (job seeker or employer)
Important: We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services and improve your experience on JobLink Kenya.

3. Third-Party Authentication

JobLink Kenya allows users to sign in using Google OAuth for secure and convenient authentication. When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive limited basic profile information from your Google account.

The information we receive includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile photo

This data is used solely for authentication, account creation, and account management purposes. JobLink Kenya does not access, read, store, or modify your Google contacts, emails, files, calendar, or any other Google services.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your JobLink Kenya account
  • Enable secure login and identity verification
  • Match job seekers with relevant job opportunities
  • Improve platform functionality and performance

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is never shared with third parties except when required by law or necessary to protect our users and platform.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Users may request account deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your account

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at:

support@stepper.dpdns.org
