Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how JobLink Kenya uses cookies and similar technologies, what they do, and how you can manage them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) that store information about your visit. They help websites remember preferences, track usage for analytics, and support functionality you expect to work when you visit again. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to provide, protect, and improve our services, including:

  • Ensuring secure login and user session management.
  • Remembering your preferences and settings.
  • Measuring how you use the site so we can improve content and performance.
  • Delivering relevant features or marketing content (where applicable).
Important: Cookies do not store your password or personal information like your full name or email, unless you provide those directly to us.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for basic site functions such as authentication and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make, like language or display preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used to deliver personalized content and measure effectiveness of promotions.

Cookies can also be categorized by duration:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or are deleted manually.

4. Third Party Cookies

We may allow third parties (such as analytics or advertising partners) to place cookies when you interact with content such as embedded videos or social media features. These cookies are governed by the third party’s own policies. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (note: this may affect site functionality).
  • Delete cookies already stored on your device.
  • Customize cookie settings per site in some browsers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated version here.
